Ada Week 3 recap

Posted on August 28 at 10:00 am

Week 3 was Bank Accounts! It was time to put together what we'd learned about classes, and add to that modules, CSV reading, and inheritance. We worked alone, but the group of students that sits near me is always willing to troubleshoot together, even if we are working at different paces.

The biggest thing I've learned at Ada thus far is how easy it is to simply ask when I don't understand a concept or am not familiar with a term. I've spent almost 28 years scared to ask questions that make me look stupid, but something about Ada has made it more okay for me to simply say, "what do you mean by that?"

We also had a great Computer Science Fundamentals lesson on Stacks and Queues with Crystal. The CS FUN classes are some of my favorites -- learning the logic and theory behind computer science is the most interesting stuff we've learned thus far.
